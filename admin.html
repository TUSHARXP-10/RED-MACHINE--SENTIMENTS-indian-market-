About This Project

The Stock Market Sentiment AI Dashboard is an advanced analytics platform that uses artificial intelligence to analyze news articles and social media content related to the Indian stock market. It provides real-time sentiment analysis, trend predictions, and market insights.

How It Helps Traders

This sentiment analysis dashboard provides traders with critical insights that can significantly improve trading decisions and strategy development. By analyzing market sentiment in real-time, traders can identify potential market movements before they occur and adjust their strategies accordingly:

Real-time Sentiment Analysis

Analyzes thousands of news articles and social media posts to determine market sentiment, helping traders gauge market psychology before making decisions.

Predictive Analytics

Uses machine learning algorithms to predict market movements based on sentiment trends, giving traders an edge in anticipating price movements.

Source Tracking

Monitors and analyzes content from over 50 financial news sources and social platforms, allowing traders to identify which sources impact market sentiment most.

Market-Aware System

Automatically adjusts analysis based on market hours, weekends, and holidays, ensuring traders always have context-appropriate insights.

Risk Management

Helps traders identify potential market volatility based on sentiment shifts, allowing for better position sizing and stop-loss placement.

Sector-Specific Insights

Provides sentiment breakdown by market sectors, helping traders identify opportunities in specific industries experiencing sentiment shifts.
